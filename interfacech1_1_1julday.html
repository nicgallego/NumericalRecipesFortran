integer function julday (mm, id, iyyy)
 

Detailed Description

converts a date to Julian number

The returned julday begins at noon of the specified calendar day.

Parameters
[in]mmmonth 01 January ... 12 December
[in]idday of the month. 1 to 31 at most
[in]iyyyyear -1 is a B.C and the next is +1, 1 A.D. There is no year 0
Returns
integer Julian day

Example: Julian Day 2440000 began at noon of May 23, 1968. Additionally jd+1 module 7 gives the day of the week. 0 Sunday, 1 Monday, ... 6 Saturday.

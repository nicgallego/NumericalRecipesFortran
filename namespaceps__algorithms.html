Detailed Description

algorithms to compute the powers of the golden mean for benchmarking

Compute the first n powers \(\phi^n\) of the "Golden-Mean"

\[ \phi \equiv \frac{\sqrt{5} - 1}{2} \approx 0.61803398 \]
